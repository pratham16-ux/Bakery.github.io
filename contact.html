Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a message above.

Do you take custom cake orders?
Absolutely! We love making celebration cakes. Please get in touch at least two weeks in advance. For large wedding cakes, we recommend reaching out 2–3 months ahead. We'll arrange a tasting session to dial in flavours and design.
Can I pre-order my bread for collection?
Yes! Order via our online shop before 6pm and collect from 7am the next morning. We'll have everything ready and waiting. Pre-orders also guarantee you get the loaves before they sell out — our sourdoughs typically go within an hour of opening.
Do you offer catering for events?
We cater for corporate breakfasts, weddings, private dinners and community events. Our catering menu includes bread and pastry boards, afternoon tea packages, and custom savoury options. Get in touch with your event date and guest count to get a quote.
Are your products suitable for vegans?
Several of our breads are naturally vegan — our sourdoughs, focaccia, rye loaf, and baguettes contain no animal products. We clearly label allergens on all products in-store. Our pastries generally contain butter and eggs, but we can advise on suitable options.
Where do you source your ingredients?
Our flours come from heritage wheat farms within 100km of London, stoneground to order. Butter is sourced from a small Charentes dairy in France. Seasonal fruit and vegetables come from Borough Market suppliers we've known for over a decade. No compromise on quality.
Do you offer bread subscriptions?
We're currently developing a weekly bread subscription service. Sign up to our newsletter to be first in line when we launch — subscribers will receive early access with a founder's discount.
Can I buy Stackly bread wholesale?
We work with a small number of restaurants, delis and cafés in South London. If you're interested in a wholesale partnership, please fill in the contact form above and select 'Wholesale' as the enquiry type. We'll send over our trade information pack.
Do you run baking classes?
Yes! Weekend sourdough workshops run on the last Saturday of each month. Spaces are strictly limited to 8 people and typically sell out within hours of release. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when new dates go live.
